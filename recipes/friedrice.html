Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Okay guys, so I'm sure you've always heard about fried rice for a quite a while now. And judging by how delicious the meal is, I can assure you the hype around it is worth it. But then, the meal is not one that's common in your localty, right, so that means you may have one or two or plenty issues with making it. So how do you go about it?

That's where I come in

So I'll be taking you through the steps involved in preparing a delicious meal of fried rice, so you just follow closely, and at the end of this tutorial, you too will be a pro in no time. But first, let's see the ingredients needed in preparing fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice (3 cups)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Stock
  • Green beans (one can, which is opened and drained in a sieve)
  • Green peas( about 15-20 pieces diced)
  • Green pepper (3 pieces cut into small pieces)
  • Carrots (about 10-15 medium sized, cut into small pieces)
  • Cabbage (shredded)
  • Curry and Thyme
  • Salt and Seasoning(to taste)
  • Sweet corn (one can, which is opened and drained in a sieve)

Steps

  1. Parboil the rice
  2. Sieve it and let the water drain
  3. Then, all the ingredients, apart from the stock, green beans and sweet corn, are fried in a small quantity of the vegetable oil
  4. The parboiled rice is then added to the fried ingredients and stirred so that the fried ingredients can mix properly with the rice.
  5. A little quantity of water is added, along with the stock, drained green beans and sweet corn'
  6. Salt and Maggi are added to taste.
  7. If desired, more diced carrot and shredded cabbage can be added while the rice cooks or after it's done.
