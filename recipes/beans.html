Beans Recipe

Description

Today, we'll be talking about how we cook beans. Mind you, we're doing it the Nigerian way. So before we get started, I'll list the ingredients required in cooking beans

Ingredients

  • Desired number of cups of beans (Picked)
  • Onion
  • Red pepper (Grinded or powdered)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Palm oil

Steps

  1. The beans is boiled in water till it's almost completely cooked.
  2. The onion is sliced into the cooking beans a couple of minutes after the beans has been poured into the pot of boiling water.
  3. When the beans is almost completely soft, a desired amount of pepper and palm oil is added.
  4. Salt is then added to taste
  5. The beans is then allowed to cook for a little more while till it's completely soft.
